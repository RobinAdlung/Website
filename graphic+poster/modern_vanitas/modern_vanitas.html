Modern Vanitas

Graphic & Poster | Vector Graphic Poster

What would a modern version of the baroque vanitas idea look like?
It would be vanitas in a meritocracy. Everyday is dedicated to work. There is no time left for the joys of life. But all efforts are in vain, considered there is no future but death. In order to distinguish the image from the Baroque, it was decided to use pure colors.

The first sketch was already planned on an isometric raster. Thus the composition of the elements is clear to recognize as a skull shape.
Also new vanitas symbols appear in the picture. For example a ToDo-List which is ripped off. Death sometimes comes very sudden. Then there is the smartphone - a device which is the main cause for all distraction in life. The opend hand symbolizes weakness but also the value of human life. It reaches out to the opened window - gasping for freedom, hope and a hereafter.
