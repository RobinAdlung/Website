Evolution of Shape

The idea behind Shape Sans is as simple as the Typeface itself. From the beginning of typography typefaces tended to develope from complex pictograms to simplifyed versions. Shape Sans reduces the schemes of the original glyphs to simple shapes - without compromises.

Behind the Design

To create this unique typeface all gaps and counters were removed. Only the recognizable shape of the glyphs remained. Therefore a balance between readability and plain shape was created. Rounded corners add to the fonts characteristic bold but soft look. The reader needs some time to recognize the glyph, which helps to create a larger awareness for the content displayed in Shape Sans. Therefore avoid using Shape Sans with long headlines or worse text, since it might confuse the reader.

Use the shapes!

Shape Sans is not just an ordinary font. Since it is made out of flat shapes, it can be used as such! This allows designers to communicate in new interesting ways.
